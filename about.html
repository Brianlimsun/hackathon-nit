About

MedEase is a website where people can make appointments with the doctor, both online and offline, record their health data, learn about the common diseases( causes, symptoms, prevention, etc), and learn to lead a healthy life by learning about the day-to-day measures of improving one’s lifestyle.

The Vision

“Quality And Convenient”

The Mission

“To bring health at the fingertips of all irrespective of gender, caste, religion, national, social status and other differences.”
